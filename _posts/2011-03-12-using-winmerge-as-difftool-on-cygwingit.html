Last couple of weeks i have been using Git</a> on cygwin</a> and i got very satisfying results out of it. One thing that i wanted to tweak was the ability to use WinMerge</a> to compare files. Here is how i do it:</p>

Here is my ~/.gitconfig:</p>

[code lang="bash"]
[user]
name = Tim Van Wassenhove
email = git@timvw.be

[diff]
external = "~/bin/git-diff-wrapper.sh"
[/code]

Here is my ~/bin/git-diff-wrapper.sh (it uses cygpath</a> to translate the paths):</p>

[code lang="bash"]
#!/bin/sh
"/cygdrive/c/Program Files/WinMerge/WinMergeU.EXE" /e /ub /dl other /dr local "`cygpath -aw $1`" "`cygpath -aw $2`" "`cygpath -aw $5`"
[/code]

And now we're good to go ;)</p>
