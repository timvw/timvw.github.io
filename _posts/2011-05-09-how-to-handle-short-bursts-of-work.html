Here is a summary of how businesses around me handle short bursts of work:</p>

  • When i go to the doctor:
    • find an empty seat in the waiting room</li>
    • observe who's before me</li>
    • wait untill all those people have been seen by the doctor</li>
      </ul>
      </li>

    • When i go to the supermarket:
      • join the queue at the end</li>
      • wait untill al before me have been helped</li>
        </ul>
        </li>

      • When i go the the bakery:
        • get a number from a ticketing machine at the entrance</li>
        • wait untill a seller indicates he's willing to process my requests</li>
          </ul>
          </li>

        • When i go to my car dealer:</li>
          • hand over car keys</li>
          • give my mobile phone number</li>
          • relax in the loung untill they come find me (or send me an sms)</li>
            </ul>
            </li>
