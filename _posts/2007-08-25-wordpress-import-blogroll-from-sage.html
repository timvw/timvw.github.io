Earlier today i exported the list with blogs i read from Sage</a> and tried to import them into WordPress</a>. Although all the entries had been processed but none of them appeared. Apparently you have to rename the xmlUrl attribute to htmlUrl in the OPML</a> file. With Vim that's as simple as ":%s/xmlUrl/htmlUrl/g".</p>

Anyway, feel free to see if you find anything interesting in the list of blogs i'm reading</a>.</p>
