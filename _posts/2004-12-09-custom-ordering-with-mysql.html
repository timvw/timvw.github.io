As a follow up to Custom Ordering</a> I discovered the nice Field</a> function in MySQL.</a> after it was mentionned on my favorite PHP Forum</a> by Weirdan</a>. It allows one to order a column on a custom order relation.</p>
[code lang="sql"]
SELECT *
FROM foo
ORDER BY FIELD(column, 'Z', 'B', 'C')
[/code]
