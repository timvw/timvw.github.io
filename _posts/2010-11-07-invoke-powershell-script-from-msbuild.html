Here is a small MSBuild target that allows you to invoke a PowerShell script, eg: powershell.exe & 'script.ps1' -SomeParam 'x'</p>

[code lang="xml"]


"$(PowerShellTool)" "&amp; '$(ScriptFile)' -SomeParam '$(SomeParam)' "</PowerShellCommand>
</PropertyGroup>

</Target>
[/code]
