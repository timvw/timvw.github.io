I've already written that i like the GNU Textutils</a> a lot. Today someone had the following problem: A textfile with words. It's possible that a word is repeated a couple of times. He wants to generate a newfile without duplicate words. The solution is pretty simple:</p>
[code lang="bash"]
sort words.txt | uniq > newfile.txt
[/code]
