Most ValueObjects</a> that i have implemented were wrappers around an int or a string. Thus i decided to create a couple of Item templates that generate this code (and related tests).</p>

Simply save IntValueObject.zip</a> and StringValueObject.zip</a> under %My Documents%\Visual Studio 2005\Templates\ItemTemplates and click on "Add New Item" in your project:</p>
