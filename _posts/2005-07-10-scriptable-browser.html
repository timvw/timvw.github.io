Last couple of days i've been trying out Simple Test</a>. It allowed me stop stop echo</a> and print_r</a> variables all over the place. The package also has a Scriptable Browser</a>.</p>

At smscity.be</a> you can earn credits each day. Therefor you have to visit their site and click some links. I wrote a smscity.txt script</a> that does this for me.</p>

Now all i had to do is make sure this script is executed each day, so i edited my crontab</a>. It looks like:</p>

[code lang="php"]###############################################################################

@reboot /usr/bin/fetchmail -d 1800
0,10,20,30,40,50 * * * * /usr/bin/wget -O /dev/null http://timvw/cron/blogmarks.php > /dev/null 2>&1
30 02 * * * /usr/bin/wget -O /dev/null http://timvw/cron/smscity.php > /dev/null 2>&1
