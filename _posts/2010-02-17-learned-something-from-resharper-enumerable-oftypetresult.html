A couple of weeks ago i discovered Enumerable.OfType</a> when i let Resharper rewrite my code as a Linq statement. Here is the original code:</p>

[code lang="csharp"]var selectedPersons = new List ();
foreach(var selectedItem in selectedItems)
{
var selectedPerson = selectedItem as PersonSelectItem;
if (selectedPerson == null) continue;
selectedPersons.Add(selectedPerson);
}[/code]

And here is how it looks after the rewrite:</p>

[code lang="csharp"]var selectedPersons = selectedItems.OfType ().ToList();[/code]

Yup, the Resharper</a> license was definitely worth it's money.</p>
