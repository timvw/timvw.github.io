Yesterday i wrote that you can use __($string, $domain) and _e($string, $domain) to internationalize a string with WordPress</a>. I forgot to mention that if you use sprintf</a> you can handle strings with variables too. An example:</p>
[code lang="php"]
echo sprintf(__('There are %d monkeys in the %s'), $domain), $number, $location);
[/code]
